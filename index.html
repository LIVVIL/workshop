Workshop organizers

Romain Vuillemot: is currently a visiting Researcher at INSA Lyon. He previously was a Research Fellow at the Center for International Development (CID) at Harvard University. He was working with economist researchers to make descriptive and predictive economic data more accessible to a global audience, ranging from decision-makers to citizens with The Atlas Economic Complexity, and has been declined to Colombian, Mexican and Peruvian economies.

Jeremy Boy: is currently a data visualization specialist at the United Nations Global Pulse New York Lab, USA.

Aurélien Tabard: is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Université Lyon 1, France.

Charles Perin: is a postdoc at the University of Calgary. His doctoral research, at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Information Visualization, focused on exploring new interaction techniques for Information Visualization.

Jean-Daniel Fekete: is a Research Director and the Aviz team leader at INRIA Saclay, France.
